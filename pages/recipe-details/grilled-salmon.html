GRILLED SALMON

Description

Grilled Salmon is a delicious and healthy dish that highlights the natural flavors of fresh salmon while adding a smoky and charred element from the grill. This recipe provides a simple and foolproof method for grilling salmon to perfection. With a few key ingredients and minimal preparation, you can enjoy a flavorful and nutritious meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (6 ounces each)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried dill
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Lemon wedges for serving
  • Fresh dill or parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, dried dill, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Place the salmon fillets in a shallow dish and pour the marinade over them, ensuring all sides are coated. Let them marinate for about 15 minutes.
  4. Lightly oil the grill grates to prevent the salmon from sticking. Place the salmon fillets on the grill, skin-side down, if applicable.
  5. Grill the salmon for about 4-6 minutes per side, depending on the thickness of the fillets, or until the salmon is opaque and flakes easily with a fork. Avoid overcooking to maintain moisture.
  6. Remove the grilled salmon from the heat and transfer to a serving platter.
  7. Garnish with fresh dill or parsley, if desired, and serve with lemon wedges on the side.

Tips

  • Ensure the grill is properly preheated to achieve those distinct grill marks and a smoky flavor.
  • If you prefer a stronger citrus flavor, you can add lemon zest to the marinade.
  • For extra flavor, you can sprinkle additional herbs or spices such as paprika or thyme on the salmon before grilling.
  • Salmon pairs well with a variety of side dishes, including roasted vegetables, steamed asparagus, or a fresh green salad.
  • Leftover grilled salmon can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 2-3 days. It's great for adding to salads or making salmon sandwiches.
