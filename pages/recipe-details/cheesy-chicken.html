CHEESY ITALIAN CHICKEN

Description

Cheesy Italian Chicken is a delicious and flavorful dish that combines tender chicken breasts, aromatic herbs, tangy marinara sauce, and gooey melted cheese. This dish captures the essence of Italian cuisine and is sure to satisfy your taste buds. It's a perfect option for a comforting and hearty meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt, black pepper, dried oregano, dried basil, garlic powder, and onion powder on both sides.
  3. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom.
  4. Place the seasoned chicken breasts on top of the sauce in a single layer.
  5. Cover each chicken breast with additional marinara sauce.
  6. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese evenly over the chicken.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for about 25-30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes.
  9. Garnish with chopped fresh basil or parsley, if desired, before serving.

Tips

  • For extra flavor, you can marinate the chicken breasts in Italian dressing for 30 minutes to 1 hour before seasoning and baking.
  • Feel free to customize the cheese blend by adding other types of Italian cheeses like provolone or Romano.
  • Serve the Cheesy Italian Chicken with pasta, rice, or a side of steamed vegetables for a complete meal.
  • Leftovers can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3-4 days. Reheat in the oven or microwave before serving.
