MOLASSES AND RAISIN BRAN MUFFINS

Description

Molasses and Raisin Bran Muffins are a delicious and nutritious breakfast or snack option. These muffins are packed with wholesome ingredients like bran flakes, molasses, and raisins, offering a delightful combination of flavors and textures. Whether enjoyed warm or at room temperature, these muffins are sure to satisfy your cravings.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups bran flakes cereal
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1/3 cup molasses
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup raisins

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Grease or line a muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the bran flakes cereal and buttermilk. Let it sit for a few minutes to allow the cereal to soften.
  3. Add the molasses, vegetable oil, brown sugar, egg, and vanilla extract to the bowl. Stir until well combined.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, ground cinnamon, and salt.
  5. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and stir until just combined.
  6. Fold in the raisins until evenly distributed throughout the batter.
  7. Spoon the muffin batter into the prepared muffin tin, filling each cup about 2/3 full.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-18 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.
  9. Remove the muffins from the oven and let them cool in the tin for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

Tips

  • You can customize the muffins by adding chopped nuts like walnuts or pecans for extra crunch.
  • For a variation, you can substitute the raisins with other dried fruits such as chopped dates, cranberries, or chopped apricots.
  • Serve the muffins warm with a spread of butter or a dollop of yogurt for a delicious treat.
  • Store the cooled muffins in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3-4 days. They can also be frozen for longer storage.
  • Feel free to sprinkle some additional bran flakes on top of the muffins before baking for added texture.
